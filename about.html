The Story So Far...

The Human Kingdom of Etheria has been captured by Orcs. The only resistance to their rule is the brave Human Vanguard : a rag-tag insurgency that wishes to restore human rule. They have chosen their two best fighters to lead a covert assasination mission deep into enemy territory...

These fighters are the last hope of humanity and possess deadly abilities. Can they bring down the Orc Warlord and his underlings or perish like dogs?

What is Incursion

A Multiplayer turn-based strategy deckbuilding game where two human champions try to conquer the captured orc-controlled province of Feldenberg. Work together with your teammates to beat the odds and defeat the Orc Warlord to take the province once and for all!

Features

Play with one other person and work together to conquer territories controlled by maurading orcs...
Players have a choice of what champion they want to be each champion, has unique special abilities their deck is based around.
Each player can choose specific relics upon victory that each have unique passive effects.
Each turn is a round of combat where cards are, used to attack the opposition.
If both players are defeated, the game is over; they have lost.
