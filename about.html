The Creation

According to legendary tales there lived an ancient tribe trained to fight, live, and kill. No trespassers lived to tell the tale; glowing orbs house the most powerful creatures of this land. A portal opened between a distant planet and, Etheria two powerful summoners entered this infested planet harnessing their power from summoning stones to lead their way to conquest.

These summoners possess powers of wielding terrible magic on the battlefield. If one does not use their magic wisely, they will be defeated, in this ceaseless battle for supremacy.

What is Incursion

A Multiplayer turn-based strategy deckbuilding game where two human champions try to conquer territories controlled by powerful creatures in the fantasy world, Etheria. Play the character of a powerful, champion and work together with your teammate to defeat the orc warlord protected by their predecessors.

Features

Play with one other person and work together to conquer territories controlled by powerful creatures.
Players have a choice of what champion they want to be each champion, has unique special abilities their deck is based around.
Each player can upgrade their deck on round victory.
Each turn is a round of combat where cards are, used to attack the opposition.
If both players are defeated, the game is over; they have lost.
